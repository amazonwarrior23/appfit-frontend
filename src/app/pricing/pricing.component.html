Getting Started

We offer 3 tracks in order to get started with us at CrossFit Endemic. Based on your sports and athletic history, as well as your CrossFit experience, we may require that you complete a series of Personal Training sessions with on of our qualified trainers, attend our Small Group Fundamentals program, or take part in a Test Out.

Personal Training

Personal training is offered to all members if they’re looking to improve upon certain skillsets or receive more in depth 1-on-1 attention. It is also an option available to anyone interested in joining our gym! The attention provided to athletes in a 1-on-1 session is unmatched, and this environment allows our personal trainers the opportunity to cater the pace of learning and development to the individual athlete. Most new members choosing this track are ready to join classes within 3 to 4 sessions with one of our certified trainers. Note: This track may be required for some depending on previous history of injuries or health issues who are interested in starting CrossFit with us.

Due to COVID-19, we are NOT currently offering our small group fundamentals. All fundamentals are being conducted in a personal training environment. They can be conducted individually OR simultaneously with close friends or family members.

Test Out

Those with CrossFit experience, have the opportunity to Test Out of the On Ramp Program. We do a basic assessment on form and how well the athlete knows the movements, as well as cater the session to the athletes needs in order to correct movement faults. *Please note: It is at the discretion of the trainer on whether the athlete passes the assessment. If not we may require the athlete to go through our On Ramp program.

Call (719)418-5463 or email ctvaan@gmail.com to set up a face to face consultation with us and get started today!
